Welcome to Challengr

What Is Challengr?

Challengr is one-part social network, one-part sports betting app. The idea behind Challengr is that sports gambling is still illegal in the USA, which pushes Americans to use a bookie or an off-shore online sports book. Challengr is simply a social network in which you can add friends and send challenges based on the week's NFL games (more sports and futures bets are on the horizon). With every challenge there is an option for a wager, which uses an arbitrary measure called units. With Challengr you can track your betting record with your friends with a tracker on how many units you are up or down. Outside of Challengr you can determine what the wager unit actually means, whether each unit counts for $1 or $5 or simply for pride. That's the beauty of Challengr, you make the rules, we provide the platform.
