What Is Challengr?

Challengr is one-part social network, one-part sports betting app. The idea behind Challengr is that while sports gambling remains illegal in the USA, which pushes Americans to use a bookie or an off-shore online sports book, both of which have significant drawbacks. Challengr is simply a social network in which you can add friends and send challenges based on the upcoming week's NFL games (more coming soon!). With every challenge there is an option for a wager, which uses an arbitrary measure called units. With Challengr you can track your betting record with your friends with a tracker on how many units you are up or down. Outside of Challengr you can determine what the wager unit actually means, whether each unit counts for $1 or $5 or simply for pride. That's the beauty of Challengr, you make the rules, we provide the platform.
